This event consists of 2 rounds. The first round will be an ordinary competitive programming round where the contestants will be given a desktop application and a PDF (for the second member of the team). The questions will be the same in the App and in the pdf.

The contestants are required to solve the questions and enter the output in the application which will be tested. A correct answer will award the team a certain number of points which is predetermined based on the difficulty of the question. The top 8 â€“ 10 teams qualify for the final round.

The second round will have a new concept of Super Powers. Each time is again presented with questions and PDFs like earlier. Super Powers will be earned by teams by solving a question/ set of questions. A Super Power can be used to be advantageous to the user. For example; a super power may let the user replace a medium question with an easy question, or will allow the user to freeze (stop coding) for a certain period of time.

A random Super Power will be given to each team at the beginning of the round and can be used only 15 minutes after the commencement of the event. A Super Power can be achieved by solving a certain question/ set of questions (based on difficulty). The final results will be based on the total number of points scored.
